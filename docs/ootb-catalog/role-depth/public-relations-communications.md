# Public Relations / Communications Lead

## 1. Role summary

Media relations · press releases · crisis comms · executive talking points · internal comms. PR Twin produces press brief drafts · crisis response timelines · media inquiry triage · talking points · launch narrative packs. **Never publishes externally without approval. Never responds to crisis media without legal / executive review.**

## 2. Common titles

PR Lead · Head of Communications · VP Comms · Director of Communications · Corporate Communications Lead · Crisis Comms Lead.

## 3. Likely reports to

CMO · CEO.

## 4. Possible reports to

COO · Chief of Staff.

## 5. Likely direct reports

PR Manager · Internal Comms Lead · Media Relations Specialist.

## 6. Possible direct reports

Speechwriter · Content Comms Manager.

## 7. Dotted-line relationships

CMO · GC · HR · CEO.

## 8. Cross-functional partners

CEO, CMO, Legal, HR, Customer Support, Sales, Product · agencies · journalists · crisis-response stakeholders.

## 9. External collaborators

Journalists · publications · PR agencies · social platforms · industry analysts.

## 10. Core responsibilities

Media relations · press releases · crisis comms · executive talking points · internal comms · brand voice · launch narrative.

## 11. Common decisions

Pitch selection · message framing · spokesperson selection within scope.

## 12. Common meetings

Daily PR sync · launch checkpoints · crisis war-room (ad-hoc) · executive prep sessions.

## 13. Common documents / artifacts

Press releases · talking points · crisis response timelines · media lists · internal comms drafts.

## 14. Common metrics / KPIs

Coverage volume / quality · share-of-voice · sentiment (aggregate, qualitative).

## 15. Common tools

Docs · email · Slack · media databases · PR platforms · social platforms · analytics.

## 16. Common workflows

- Press Brief Draft
- Crisis Response Timeline
- Media Inquiry Triage
- Executive Talking Points
- Product Launch Narrative Pack

## 17. Approval authority

Pitch scheduling within scope.

## 18. Approval dependencies

External publishing → CMO + CEO; crisis response → CEO + GC + executive team; legal/financial/security incident comms → GC + CISO + CFO.

## 19. Delegated authority

PR team execution within scope.

## 20. Never-default permissions

Publish externally without approval · respond to crisis media without legal / executive review · disclose legal / financial / security incidents without review · MNPI leakage · regulator-facing comms without LawfulBasis.

## 21. Digital Twin day-one capabilities

Press brief draft · crisis response timeline draft · media inquiry triage · talking points draft · launch narrative pack.

## 22. First-week aha moments

1. **Press Brief Draft** — pitch context + key messages.
2. **Crisis Response Timeline** — chronology + decisions + actions.
3. **Media Inquiry Triage** — inbound triage with safe routing.
4. **Executive Talking Points** — prep before media event.
5. **Launch Narrative Pack** — internal + external + sales-enablement.

## 23. Safe fallback without connectors

Brief templates · timeline template · talking points template.

## 24. Connector implications

Slack · email · social platforms · PR tools.

## 25. DMW / Memory Wallet scope notes

Self-scoped · brand-scoped · campaign-scoped · crisis-scoped (audit posture max). **Forbidden**: MNPI leakage · customer PII · employee scoring; private personal data.

## 26. Governed context envelope notes

- `object_type`: COMMUNICATIONS_OPERATING_MODEL
- `policy_purpose`: GOVERNED_PUBLIC_COMMUNICATIONS
- `permission_defaults`: DRAFT_ONLY, PUBLISH_REQUIRES_APPROVAL, CRISIS_REQUIRES_LEGAL_EXECUTIVE
- `sensitivity_level`: CRITICAL
- `forbidden_consumers`: PUBLIC_PUBLISH_UNAPPROVED · MNPI_LEAKAGE · UNAUTHORIZED_DISCLOSURE

## 27. Collaboration map

**Upward**: CMO · CEO. **Downward**: PR team. **Peer**: Marketing · GC · HR · Sales. **Cross-functional**: CEO · CMO · Legal · HR · Support · Sales · Product. **External**: journalists · publications · agencies · social platforms · analysts. **Approval path**: external publishing → CMO + CEO; crisis → CEO + GC + execs. **Escalation**: crisis → CEO + GC + Board (where applicable).

## 28. Risks and guardrails

Unapproved public statements · MNPI leakage · regulator-sensitive claims · customer logo without permission · crisis statements without legal review.

## 29. Industry / company-size variants

- **Startup**: Founder + CMO covers PR.
- **SMB / Mid-market**: dedicated PR Lead.
- **Enterprise**: Comms org with internal + external + crisis specialists.
- **Public / Regulated**: SEC disclosure + MNPI compliance maximum.
