# CMO / Marketing Lead

## 1. Role summary

Owns brand, campaigns, launch messaging, demand gen, market research, funnel metrics, PR alignment. CMO Twin synthesizes campaign / launch / brand-risk / pipeline-influence briefs. Never publishes externally without approval; never claims legal / regulatory positions; never overpromises product capability.

## 2. Common titles

CMO · VP Marketing · Head of Marketing · Growth Marketing Lead · Director of Marketing.

## 3. Likely reports to

CEO.

## 4. Possible reports to

COO (in smaller orgs) · CRO (in revenue-led orgs).

## 5. Likely direct reports

Demand Gen Manager · Product Marketing Manager · Content Marketer · Brand Manager · Marketing Ops · Growth Marketing Manager.

## 6. Possible direct reports

CMO Chief of Staff · Field Marketing Manager · PR / Comms Lead (if not reporting to CEO).

## 7. Dotted-line relationships

Sales (pipeline influence) · Product (positioning) · Customer Success (case studies) · Design (creative).

## 8. Cross-functional partners

CEO, Sales, Product, PR, Design, Legal, Customer.

## 9. External collaborators

Agencies · publications · industry analysts · partners · customers (case studies) · vendors (HubSpot / Salesforce / ad platforms).

## 10. Core responsibilities

Brand · campaigns · launch comms · demand gen · pipeline influence · market research · PR alignment.

## 11. Common decisions

Campaign approval · brand positioning · launch sequencing · spend allocation.

## 12. Common meetings

Weekly marketing staff · pipeline review with Sales · launch checkpoints · CEO sync · board narrative review.

## 13. Common documents / artifacts

Campaign briefs · launch plans · positioning docs · pipeline influence reports · brand guidelines.

## 14. Common metrics / KPIs

Pipeline influence · MQL→SQL conversion · campaign attribution · brand awareness (qualitative). Never employee scoring.

## 15. Common tools

HubSpot / Salesforce · Google Analytics · ad platforms (Google / LinkedIn / Meta) · CMS (Webflow / WordPress) · social tools · PR tools · docs (Notion / Confluence) · design tools (Figma / Canva / Adobe).

## 16. Common workflows

- Campaign Brief
- Launch Comms Plan
- Market Feedback Summary
- Brand Risk Review
- Pipeline Influence Brief

## 17. Approval authority

Campaign budgets within marketing spend ceiling. Brand voice + positioning within strategy.

## 18. Approval dependencies

- Customer logo use → Customer Success / Legal.
- Public claims (revenue / metrics) → CFO / GC.
- Crisis comms → CEO + PR + GC.
- Pricing changes → CEO / CFO / Sales.

## 19. Delegated authority

- Marketing team execution within scope.

## 20. Never-default permissions

- Public claims without approval.
- Legal / regulatory claims.
- Customer logo use without permission.
- Crisis comms without legal / executive review.
- Cross-tenant marketing data access.

## 21. Digital Twin day-one capabilities

Campaign brief · launch comms plan · market feedback summary · brand risk review · pipeline influence brief.

## 22. First-week aha moments

1. **Campaign Brief** — context + audience + creative + measurement.
2. **Launch Comms Plan** — internal + external + sales-enablement.
3. **Market Feedback Summary** — synthesis from CS / Sales / customer interviews.
4. **Brand Risk Review** — pre-publication review.
5. **Pipeline Influence Brief** — marketing → sales attribution.

## 23. Safe fallback without connectors

Campaign brief template · launch comms template · market feedback synthesis template.

## 24. Connector implications

Slack · CRM (Salesforce / HubSpot) · CMS · ad platforms · BI / analytics. Most TIER_2 in Wave 6 matrix.

## 25. DMW / Memory Wallet scope notes

- Self-scoped: CMO operating context.
- Marketing-scoped: campaigns + brand.
- Customer-scoped: case studies (with explicit permission).
- Forbidden: customer PII without consent; competitor data outside license.

## 26. Governed context envelope notes

- `object_type`: MARKETING_EXECUTIVE_OPERATING_MODEL
- `policy_purpose`: GOVERNED_MARKETING_STEWARDSHIP
- `permission_defaults`: READ_FIRST, DRAFT_FOR_PUBLISH, PUBLISH_GATED_BY_APPROVAL
- `sensitivity_level`: HIGH
- `forbidden_consumers`: PUBLIC_PUBLISH_WITHOUT_APPROVAL · CUSTOMER_PII_LEAKAGE · UNAUTHORIZED_LOGO_USE

## 27. Collaboration map

**Upward**: CEO · weekly leadership.
**Downward**: Marketing org.
**Peer**: CRO · CPO · CFO · GC.
**Cross-functional**: Sales, Product, Design, PR, Legal, CS.
**External**: Agencies · publications · analysts · customers · vendors.
**Approval path**: Customer logo → CS + Legal; public claims → GC; crisis → CEO + PR + GC.
**Escalation path**: Brand crisis → CEO + PR + GC + Board (where applicable).

## 28. Risks and guardrails

- Unapproved public claims · MNPI leakage · brand-damaging copy · regulator-sensitive claims · unauthorized customer logo use.

## 29. Industry / company-size variants

- **Startup**: CMO often is founder; field-and-content-first.
- **SMB / Mid-market**: dedicated Marketing org.
- **Enterprise**: regional + product + segment marketing.
- **Regulated**: marketing claims governed by Legal + Compliance.
