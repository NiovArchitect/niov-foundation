# Customer Success Manager / Support Lead

## 1. Role summary

CS/Support: health scores · escalations · renewal risks · tickets · implementations · support quality. Twin produces customer health briefs · renewal risk summaries · support escalation summaries · implementation readiness checklists. **Never promises roadmap · never admits liability · never discloses private account data.**

## 2. Common titles

CSM · Senior CSM · Strategic CSM · Support Lead · Support Manager · Implementation Manager · Solutions Engineer · Professional Services Consultant.

## 3. Likely reports to

CS Manager → VP Customer Success / VP Support → Chief Customer Officer / CEO.

## 4. Possible reports to

CRO (in revenue-led orgs) · COO.

## 5. Likely direct reports

Support Lead → Support Agents. CSM Lead → CSMs.

## 6. Possible direct reports

Implementation specialists · technical support engineers.

## 7. Dotted-line relationships

AE (for top accounts) · Product (feedback) · Engineering (escalations).

## 8. Cross-functional partners

AE · Product · Engineering · Support · Marketing (case studies).

## 9. External collaborators

Customers · implementation partners · advocacy contacts.

## 10. Core responsibilities

CSM: account health · renewal coordination · expansion enablement · escalation. Support: ticket triage · escalations · response quality · implementation readiness.

## 11. Common decisions

Escalation routing · expansion play timing · ticket priority.

## 12. Common meetings

Weekly account reviews · QBR · ticket queue review · escalation calls.

## 13. Common documents / artifacts

Account plans · QBR decks · ticket logs · escalation summaries · case studies (with permission).

## 14. Common metrics / KPIs

NPS · CSAT · GRR / NRR · ticket-first-response · CSM-scoped pipeline.

## 15. Common tools

Zendesk · Intercom · Freshdesk · Gainsight · ChurnZero · Salesforce / HubSpot · Slack · Gmail.

## 16. Common workflows

- Customer Health Brief
- Renewal Risk Summary
- Support Escalation Summary
- Implementation Readiness Checklist

## 17. Approval authority

Routine account activity. Standard expansion plays.

## 18. Approval dependencies

Custom SLA → GC · custom pricing → Sales / Finance · roadmap promises → Product · executive escalation → VP Customer + CRO.

## 19. Delegated authority

CSM-scope only.

## 20. Never-default permissions

Promise roadmap · admit liability · disclose private account data · expose customer PII · bypass support escalation policy · expose churn risk externally.

## 21. Digital Twin day-one capabilities

Customer health brief · renewal risk summary · support escalation summary · implementation readiness checklist.

## 22. First-week aha moments

1. **Customer Health Snapshot** — CSM accounts + signals.
2. **Renewal Risk Brief** — upcoming renewals at risk.
3. **Support Escalation Summary** — daily triage.
4. **Implementation Readiness Checklist** — new deployments.
5. **QBR Prep Brief** — pre-QBR context.

## 23. Safe fallback without connectors

Health brief template · renewal risk template · escalation summary template.

## 24. Connector implications

Zendesk · Intercom · Gainsight · CRM. Wave 6: Support Platform 6.63.

## 25. DMW / Memory Wallet scope notes

Self-scoped CS context · account-scoped · ticket-scoped · implementation-scoped. **Forbidden**: customer PII exposure outside scope · cross-customer data · churn risk shared externally.

## 26. Governed context envelope notes

- `object_type`: CUSTOMER_SUCCESS_OPERATING_MODEL
- `policy_purpose`: GOVERNED_CUSTOMER_SUPPORT
- `permission_defaults`: READ_FIRST_CALLER_ACCOUNTS, DRAFT_FOR_CUSTOMER_RESPONSE
- `sensitivity_level`: HIGH
- `forbidden_consumers`: CUSTOMER_PII_EXTERNAL · CHURN_RISK_EXTERNAL · LIABILITY_ADMISSION

## 27. Collaboration map

**Upward**: CS Manager → VP Customer / CEO. **Downward**: Support Agents (Support Lead). **Peer**: AE · Product · Engineering · Marketing. **Cross-functional**: Product (feedback) · Engineering (escalations) · Sales (expansion). **External**: customers · implementation partners. **Approval path**: SLA → GC; pricing → Sales / Finance; roadmap → Product. **Escalation**: customer crisis → VP Customer + CRO + GC.

## 28. Risks and guardrails

Roadmap promises · liability admission · churn risk exposure · customer PII leakage.

## 29. Industry / company-size variants

- **Startup**: AE doubles as CSM.
- **SMB / Mid-market**: dedicated CSM per segment.
- **Enterprise**: full CSM + Support + Implementation orgs.
- **Regulated**: PHI / PII handling tightly governed.
