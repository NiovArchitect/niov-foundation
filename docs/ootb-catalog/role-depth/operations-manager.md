# Operations Manager

## 1. Role summary

Operating cadence · vendor / facility / supply-chain coordination · business operations. Twin produces operational rollup briefs · vendor renewal briefs · facility coordination updates · supply-chain risk summaries. **Never auto-commits vendors. Never employee scoring.**

## 2. Common titles

Operations Manager · Business Operations Manager · BizOps Lead · Facilities Manager · Supply Chain Manager · Vendor Manager · Director of Operations.

## 3. Likely reports to

COO · VP Operations · CFO (in finance-led orgs).

## 4. Possible reports to

CEO (smaller orgs).

## 5. Likely direct reports

Operations Analyst · Facilities Coordinator · Vendor Specialist · Supply Chain Specialist.

## 6. Possible direct reports

Office Manager · BizOps Analyst.

## 7. Dotted-line relationships

CFO (vendor spend) · CHRO (facilities) · GC (vendor contracts).

## 8. Cross-functional partners

COO · CFO · CHRO · GC · IT · Department heads.

## 9. External collaborators

Vendors · landlord · suppliers · service providers · facilities partners.

## 10. Core responsibilities

Operating cadence · vendor management · facility / supply-chain coordination · operational efficiency · vendor renewals · risk management.

## 11. Common decisions

Vendor selection within ceiling · operational improvements · facility coordination · supply-chain risk responses.

## 12. Common meetings

Weekly ops review · monthly vendor reviews · quarterly business reviews.

## 13. Common documents / artifacts

Vendor list · facility plans · operational KPI dashboards · risk log · supply-chain status.

## 14. Common metrics / KPIs

Operational efficiency · vendor spend · facility utilization · supply-chain on-time delivery.

## 15. Common tools

Coupa · NetSuite · DocuSign · ServiceNow · Slack / Teams · Google Workspace / M365 · Asana / Monday.

## 16. Common workflows

- Operational Rollup Brief
- Vendor Renewal Brief
- Facility Coordination Update
- Supply-Chain Risk Summary
- Operational Improvement Brief

## 17. Approval authority

Vendor approvals within ceiling · facility coordination within scope.

## 18. Approval dependencies

Vendor spend above ceiling → CFO. Contract terms → GC. Supply-chain commitments → COO / CFO.

## 19. Delegated authority

Operations team execution within scope.

## 20. Never-default permissions

Auto-commit vendors · auto-renew above ceiling · employee scoring · cross-tenant signals.

## 21. Digital Twin day-one capabilities

Operational rollup brief · vendor renewal brief · facility coordination update · supply-chain risk summary.

## 22. First-week aha moments

1. **Operational Rollup Brief** — weekly.
2. **Vendor Renewal Brief** — upcoming renewals.
3. **Facility Coordination Update** — coordination ahead of events.
4. **Supply-Chain Risk Summary** — risk + mitigation.
5. **Operational Improvement Brief** — efficiency suggestions.

## 23. Safe fallback without connectors

Operational rollup template · vendor renewal template · facility update template.

## 24. Connector implications

Coupa · NetSuite · DocuSign · ServiceNow · Slack.

## 25. DMW / Memory Wallet scope notes

Self-scoped · operations-scoped · vendor-scoped · facility-scoped. Forbidden: vendor confidential outside scope; employee performance signals.

## 26. Governed context envelope notes

- `object_type`: OPERATIONS_OPERATING_MODEL
- `policy_purpose`: GOVERNED_OPERATIONS_STEWARDSHIP
- `permission_defaults`: READ_FIRST_OPERATIONS, WRITE_VENDOR_APPROVAL_GATED
- `sensitivity_level`: HIGH
- `forbidden_consumers`: AUTO_VENDOR_COMMIT · EMPLOYEE_SCORING_TOOL

## 27. Collaboration map

**Upward**: COO · VP Ops · CFO. **Downward**: Ops Analyst · Facilities · Vendor specialists. **Peer**: Finance · HR · GC · IT. **Cross-functional**: all departments. **External**: vendors · landlord · suppliers · service providers. **Approval path**: vendor spend → CFO; contracts → GC. **Escalation**: supply-chain crisis → COO + CFO.

## 28. Risks and guardrails

Auto-vendor-commit · supply-chain over-commit · facility security · vendor PII exposure.

## 29. Industry / company-size variants

- **Startup**: COO + Office Manager combined.
- **SMB / Mid-market**: dedicated Ops Manager.
- **Enterprise**: Ops org with multi-region coordination.
- **Manufacturing**: heavy supply-chain + facility focus.
